Introduction About The School

A. HISTORICAL BACKGROUND :

Ramkot Basic Level School is located in Ward No. 7, Chandrakot Rural Municipality, Gulmi District, Lumbini Province of Nepal. This school is playing an important role for educational awareness in this field. The school is characterized by keeping pace with the changing times and following the aspirations of the common parents. The Ramkot Basic Level School was established in the year 2065 BS as a result of the historical, cultural and tourist area. Upon arrival, according to the decision of the District Education Office, English medium education was started up to class 1. Again, the District Education Office has decided to conduct class 5 from 2062/12/05 . School has been conducting English medium education since its inception. Computer education was conducted from class 3-4 from 2068 BS and computer education was also conducted in class 5 from 2072/12/5. The school is still pursuing its development journey by conducting technical classes and emphasizing on English medium classes.

B. Geographical Location:

This school is located in ward no. 7 Siddhababa Temple in the east, Shivalaya Temple in the west, Ramkot Community Forest in the north and Rudravati river in the south. Ramkot Basic Level School is located at an altitude of 500 feet above the Rudravati river Spread over an area of ​​1 ropani, this school is in a naturally beautiful and safe place. There are 3 buildings in this school.

C. Background of school community:

Chandrakot Rural municipality ward no. 7 Ramkot Pvt. Ltd. located in Rupakot Damuka. Multi-ethnic, multi-lingual people live around Especially Brahmins, Chhetri, Dalits and Janajatis are the majority of the people who teach at Chhayas Ramkot Pvt. Brahmins, Chhetris, Dalits and Janajatis.

D. Program Description:

With the active participation of all parents, intellectuals, social workers and teachers, the school has conducted English medium and technical education (computer) classes from class 1.1 to class four and then class 5. The school is also conducting extracurricular activities every Friday to encourage the talents of the students. With the objective of enhancing the educational quality of the school, it has been able to achieve good results by conducting examinations from time to time. Assessing the poor condition of the building in the school, good maintenance of the building and provision of kindergarten has also been made.
