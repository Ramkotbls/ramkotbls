Introduction to the school :

A. HISTORICAL BACKGROUND Ramkot Primary School is located in Ward No. 7, Chandrakot Gaonpalika, Gulmi District, Lumbini Zone, Province No. 5 of Nepal. This school is playing an important role for educational awareness in this field The school is characterized by keeping pace with the changing times and following the aspirations of the common parents. The Ramkot Primary School was established in the year 2065 BS as a result of the historical, cultural and tourist area. Upon arrival, according to the decision of the District Education Office, English medium education was started up to class 1. Again, the District Education Office has decided to conduct class 5 from 2062/12/5 and Kramas School has been conducting English medium education since its inception. Computer education was conducted from class 3-4 from 2068 BS and computer education was also conducted in class 5 from 2072/12/5. The school is still pursuing its development journey by conducting technical classes and emphasizing on English medium classes.

B. Geographical Location 7 Siddhababa temple in the east, Shivalaya temple in the west. It is located between Ramkot Community Forest in the north and Rudravati river in the south. Ramkot Primary School is located at an altitude of 500 feet above Rudravati river. Spread over an area of ​​1 ropani, the school is located in a naturally beautiful and safe place.
